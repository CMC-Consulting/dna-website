### **2. Move Beyond “What to Sell” and Focus on “How to Sell”**

Strategy, powered by AI-driven analysis, is the ultimate differentiator. While competitors are still focused on *what to sell* (for example, sourcing sustainable footwear or chasing trending products), winning businesses focus on *how to sell*.

This is where an AI synthesis tool like Prisma truly shines. Consider a hypothetical scenario for a footwear seller:

1. **Upload data sources:** The seller uploads multiple documents into Prisma, including the “2025 Footwear Business Trends” report, Shopee’s “Fixed Fee Table,” and Lazada’s “Sales Fee Schedule.”  
2. **Ask a strategic question:** Instead of reading each document manually, they ask the AI directly:  
   *“Based on these trends and fee structures, which product line (multi-purpose sneakers or leather shoes) would generate the best profit margin on Shopee Mall? Suggest a pricing strategy.”*

Within seconds, the AI cross-analyzes the documents and delivers a strategic answer:

**_AI Analysis:_** *Multi-purpose sneakers have 15% higher net profit potential on Shopee Mall. Although the fashion category’s fixed fee (13.4%) is higher than for leather shoes, the ‘multi-purpose’ trend (VENASS report) supports a 25% higher pricing level, offsetting the extra costs.*  
**_Suggested Strategy:_** *Set the price at 799,000 VND, targeting office workers who need shoes suitable for both work and light exercise.*

This process shifts a seller from a reactive position (chasing trends) to a proactive one (building a sustainable business model), using data to create an advantage—not just to survive.

### **4. “Grounded AI”: A Shield Against Errors and Hallucinations**

One of the biggest—and most overlooked—advantages of next-generation synthesis tools is the concept of **Grounded AI**. This is the foundation of trust.

Grounded AI is clearly defined: it is an AI model that generates responses **entirely and exclusively based on the source documents you provide**. It does not pull information from its general training data or from the internet. Prisma does not use public training data or web sources during analysis; it strictly analyzes and cites only user-provided documents.

Think of it this way: You would never ask Google Search or a general AI chatbot, “Based on my internal report, what should we do?”—because they don’t even know that report exists. With Grounded AI, you are effectively speaking with an expert who has read and memorized only your documents. Every answer comes from facts you supply, not from internet assumptions.

Why is this such a huge advantage? It completely eliminates the risk of AI “hallucinations” — making up information. Every analysis, summary, and strategic recommendation is guaranteed to be based on *your* trusted data. Moreover, each response includes source citations, making it easy to trace and verify information, giving you full confidence when making critical business decisions.
